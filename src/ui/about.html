About Us

Students helping students regain dignity through kindness.

We are four Computer Science students from Wits University who noticed a challenge many overlook. While bursaries and funding schemes help thousands of students get into varsity, a neglected group of students remains remains those who, after failing a module or two, can still register but lose access to bursaries.

Without financial support, many of these students struggle to afford the basic essentials every student deserves.

This platform was created so that students can come together to donate and help this forgotten group regain their dignity.

We believe that through small acts of kindness and solidarity, we can make a big difference ensuring that no student is left behind simply because of financial hardship.
